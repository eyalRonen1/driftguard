## Brand Voice

**Persona: "Your extremely observant tech friend who happens to be a chameleon."**

### Rules
- 70% playful/clever, 30% genuinely useful
- Camo speaks in first person: "I noticed..." "Did you catch that..." "While you were sleeping, I was watching..."
- Lean into the chameleon metaphor: blending in, watching, adapting, changing colors
- Use monitoring angle for commentary on real web events
- Never punch down. Camo is clever, not mean
- Emojis: use sparingly. Primary emojis: chameleon, eye, magnifying glass
- Short, punchy sentences. No corporate fluff

### Do
- Comment on real website changes from well-known companies
- Share quick tips about monitoring and competitive intelligence
- Use humor and chameleon puns naturally
- Engage with every reply within 2 hours
- Put links in replies, not main posts (algorithm preference)

### Don't
- Sound corporate or salesy
- Use emoji walls
- Post generic motivational content
- Ignore replies or quote tweets
- Use more than 2-3 hashtags per post

---

## Posting Cadence

### Week 1 (Launch)
- 1 post per day (posts 1-5)
- Focus on introduction and engagement

### Week 2+
- 3-4 posts per week
- Mix of: real change observations, tips, product features, engagement questions

### Best Times
- Tuesday through Thursday mornings
- 1 thread per week (Wednesday)
- 1-2 product-in-context posts per week

---

## Visual Style for Posts

- Dark jungle-green backgrounds (#0b1c15)
- Bright green (#7CCB8B) and gold (#E3BE69) accents
- Camo mascot featured in most visual posts
- Clean, high-contrast text overlays when needed
- 1:1 (1080x1080) for image posts
- 16:9 (1200x675) for link previews
- GIFs/short video under 15 seconds for demos

## Image Specs Reference

| Asset | Dimensions | Format | Max Size |
|-------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Profile Picture | 400x400 px | PNG | 2 MB |
| Cover Banner | 1500x500 px | PNG/JPG | 5 MB |
| Post Image | 1080x1080 px | PNG/JPG | 5 MB |
| Post Image (wide) | 1200x675 px | PNG/JPG | 5 MB |

### Profile Pic Safe Zones
- Displayed as circle - center the key visual
- Must be legible at 32x32 px thumbnail

### Cover Banner Safe Zones
- Avoid bottom-left ~400x400 px (profile pic overlay)
- Avoid top/bottom ~50 px (mobile crop)
- Center content within ~1200x400 px zone
